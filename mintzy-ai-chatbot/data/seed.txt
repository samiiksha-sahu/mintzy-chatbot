Seed FAQs
What exactly is Seed and how does it work?
Seed is an AI native forecasting and market intelligence infrastructure designed for predictive analytics, quantitative workflows, and structured decision support. It combines advanced machine learning with real-time market data to deliver behavioral and liquidity aware forecasts across multiple asset classes.
How reliable are Seed forecasting models?
Forecasts are generated through adaptive models continuously responding to evolving volatility, liquidity movement, and changing market behavior. Accuracy varies based on market conditions, timeframe, and model conviction. Each forecast includes confidence scores reflecting model certainty under current conditions.
Which markets and asset classes are supported?
The infrastructure supports Equity, Derivatives, and Crypto markets through one connected forecasting environment. Access forecasting across 500+ Indian equities, indices, commodities, and cryptocurrencies with synchronized cross-market analysis.
How frequently are predictions and forecasts updated?
Forecasting outputs update continuously based on selected intervals and evolving live market conditions. The system operates in real-time during trading hours with adaptive learning that automatically recalibrates based on new market data.
What trading or investing timeframes are available?
Seed supports intraday, swing, and multi-day analytical workflows across different market structures. From 1-minute to monthly timeframes — predictions work best on 5-minute to hourly charts for intraday and daily to weekly for swing trades.
What do confidence scores actually indicate?
Confidence scores reflect model conviction strength under changing volatility and liquidity environments. Scores range from 0-100%, with above 75% indicating strong conviction, 50-75% moderate confidence, and below 50% weak signals. Confidence helps you filter low-conviction predictions.
