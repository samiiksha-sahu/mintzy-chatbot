The Mintzy Guarantee
We take 10% of profits only. We also cover 10% of losses. We don't make money unless you do.
What is the Plugin Framework?
Mintzy's core subscription product. It gives traders automated access to AI-driven swing trading signals, hedging execution and capital protection tools through a plug-in connected to your existing broker.
How does the 10% profit share work?
At the end of each billing cycle Mintzy takes 10% of net realized profits generated through the plugin. No profits means no profit share charge.
What does 10% loss coverage mean?
If the plugin results in a net loss Mintzy covers 10% of that loss. We have skin in the game. We are incentivized to perform — not just to charge.
What is automated swing trading?
The plugin automates identification, entry and exit of medium-term directional trades within predefined risk parameters — so you benefit without monitoring positions manually.
What is the capital protection framework?
Automated risk rules built into the engine — drawdown limits, position sizing controls and hedge triggers — designed to protect capital from outsized losses in adverse conditions.
How many scripts can I trade at once?
Mini covers 500+ scripts, Alpha covers 2000+ and Beta covers the full multi-asset universe. The engine operates on scripts where model confidence is highest.
Do I stay in control?
Yes. You can pause, review or override the plugin at any time. Mintzy has no withdrawal access to your account — it only operates within the trade permissions you grant via your broker's API.
Can I cancel?
Yes at any time. Pending profit share or loss coverage is settled at the end of your current billing period.
