FROM THE FOUNDERS
Why we built Mintzy
We started Mintzy because we saw a gap between the sophistication of institutional investment research and what everyday investors could actually access. Markets move fast, data is abundant, but turning that data into confident decisions still felt out of reach for most people.
Our goal is simple: bring the rigor of quantitative research and adaptive AI forecasting to every investor — without the complexity. Every product we ship, from our plugin terminal to Seed and API, is built with that mission in mind.
Thank you for being part of this journey. We're just getting started.
