Frequently Asked Questions
What is Mintzy?
Mintzy is an AI-powered market intelligence platform giving traders and institutions access to personalized strategies and automated trading tools through one broker-independent ecosystem.
Who is Mintzy for?
Two audiences: young professionals (22–35) who want to plug-and-play financial insights and professional traders or brokerage firms that need robust API solutions built for their systems.
Do I need to switch brokers?
No. Mintzy is fully broker independent. It connects seamlessly with Zerodha, Dhan, Upstox, Angel One and more via universal APIs.
How accurate is Mintzy's intelligence?
Our adaptive AI models deliver 86–96% directional accuracy. The system uses continuous regime-learning that adapts to changing market conditions, so performance doesn't degrade over time.
What markets does Mintzy cover?
Equity, equity derivatives and crypto. Coverage scales from 500+ scripts at entry tier to full multi-asset coverage at the advanced tier.
Is Mintzy SEBI-compliant?
Yes. Every insight, alert and recommendation is SEBI-compliant. Regulatory alignment is a core design principle.
What does API pricing look like?
100 free API calls to start. Beyond that usage is priced at ₹3 per call transparent and scalable.
What is the Plugin Framework?
A subscription suite giving you access to equity scripts, automated swing trading, hedging and capital protection tools. Plans start at ₹3L/year.
How does Mintzy make money if clients lose?
It doesn't. Mintzy runs on a 10% profit share plus 10% loss coverage model. We win only when you win.
Is there a free trial?
Yes. API trials are available so you can test Mintzy's intelligence layer before committing to a plan.
